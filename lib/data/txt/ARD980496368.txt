Background
The 14-acre Industrial Waste Control (IWC) site is located in Sebastian County, Arkansas.  IWC operations included a landfill and surface impoundments and the facility received industrial waste, including wood shavings, miscellaneous rubbish and drummed solvents, from industrial plants in and around Fort Smith.

What Has Been Done to Clean Up the Site?
The site remedy included: off-site disposal of liquid-filled drums; solidification/stabilization of contaminated soil; installation of a slurry wall, french drain and multi-layer cap; ground water monitoring; and institutional controls.The site remedy included: off-site disposal of liquid-filled drums; solidification/stabilization of contaminated soil; installation of a slurry wall, french drain and multi-layer cap; ground water monitoring; and institutional controls.

What Is the Current Site Status?
EPA continues to conduct five-year reviews of the site remedy.  These reviews ensure that the remedies put in place continues to protect human health and the environment.
