Background
The Rogers Road Municipal Landfill site is about one acre of the ten-acre landfill located in a wooded, rural setting outside the city limits of Jacksonville in Pulaski County, Arkansas. The landfill is situated immediately east of Rogers Road, one-tenth mile south of Graham Road. The site is approximately 12 miles northeast of Little Rock, Arkansas.
